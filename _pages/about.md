Hello 👋 ! I'm a first year MSCSE student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My interests lie in ML/AI, specifically in Natural Language Processing. My current research at the [Language and Information Technologies (LIT) Lab](https://lit.eecs.umich.edu/) explores retrieval-augmented generation techniques to support cancer patients in navigating their healthcare journey and preparing for medical consultations.

I was previously at Caterpillar, where I worked on sensor and physics simulations for autonomous mining vehicles. As a developer, I also supported automation testing of a fleet management application and systems engineering for mine site software.

I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the College of Engineering, Guindy in 2023.

Outside of work, I am a passionate Carnatic vocalist. Stay tuned for my music page!
