Research

TexAVi: Generating Stereoscopic VR Video Clips from Text Descriptions

Shruti Jayaraman*, Bhavya R, Vriksha Srihari, Mary Anita Rajam V

IEEE CVMI 2024


This paper introduces a method for creating immersive stereoscopic VR videos from text descriptions. We leverage a text-to-image model to establish the context, then apply Stable Diffusion to enhance visual quality and realism. Through depth estimation algorithms, we generate separate views for each eye, combining them side-by-side to produce an immersive stereoscopic VR experience.
